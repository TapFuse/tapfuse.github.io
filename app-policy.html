What Information Do We Collect About You?

The information, and type of information we collect from you depends on your specific interaction with the App. To register and use the App, we require and will request your email address. When logging in, with your permission we will send you a temporary code confirming your email address. Following this step, you can choose which social networks, such as LinkedIn or Facebook, you would like to link to your account and profile. The only data that is drawn from the Social media site is your photo which is added to your app profile. See below for information we collect from your social media accounts.

You have the option to enhance your profile by adding additional information such as your date of birth, demographic information, such as your age and gender, or information from other social networks. Tapfuse also collects contact information and any other data you choose to submit when you contact us for the purpose of customer services, or to otherwise interact with the App.

Information We Collect From Third Parties.

Tapfuse may collect information about you from third parties, such as companies and groups that organize conferences and events. This may include event registration information, such as: name; job title; employer name; address; email address; work history; education history; other professionals and organizations you may be connected to or affiliated with.

 

Information that We Collect About You from Additional Social Networking Sites

With your permission, we may also access information about you from other social network accounts, including LinkedIn, Facebook, Meetup, or Eventbrite. If so, we obtain information from these sites as follows:

LinkedIn. If you provide us access to your LinkedIn account, you must enter your LinkedIn email address and password. By providing us with access to your LinkedIn account, you are granting us access to your profile information and connections.

Facebook. If you provide us access to your Facebook account, you must enter your Facebook email address and password. We will ask that you grant us permission to: (i) access and collect your Facebook basic information (this includes your name, profile picture, gender, networks, user IDs, list of friends, date of birth, email address, job title, your “likes” and any other information you have set to public on your Facebook account); (ii) provide you with material to post to your Wall; and (iii) access your Facebook data at any time even if you are not using Facebook. If you allow us to have access to this information, then we will have access to this information even if you have chosen not to make that information public.

Meetup. If you provide us access to your Meetup account, you must enter your Meetup email address and password. By logging in through Meetup, you are granting us access to the events that you have attended or will be attending.

Eventbrite. If you provide us access to your Eventbrite account, you must enter your Eventbrite email address and password. By logging in through Eventbrite, you are granting us access to the events that you have attended or will be attending.

We store the information that we receive from social networking sites with other information that we collect from you or receive about you.

Any third-party social networking site controls the information it collects from you. For information about how they may use and disclose your information, including any information you make public, please consult their respective privacy policies. We have no control over how any third party site uses or discloses the personal information it collects about you.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information, including your personal information, for the following purposes:

  • To connect you with the event stakeholders and allow you to contact them through the App.
  • To provide you with your general and personalised agenda’s.
  • To help event organizers, event sponsors, and select third parties identify the event registrants who attend the event.
  • To provide our services to you, to communicate with you about your use of our services, to respond to your inquiries, and for other customer service purposes.
  • To tailor the content and information that we may send or display to you, to offer location customization, and personalized help and instructions, and to otherwise personalize your experiences while using the App.
  • For marketing and promotional purposes. For example, we may use your information, such as your email address, to send in-app advertisements or send you push notifications.
  • To better understand how users access and use the App, both on an aggregated and individualized basis, in order to improve our App and respond to user desires and preferences, and for other research and analytical purposes, including research surveys.
  • If you have granted us permission, to post to your LinkedIn activity feed, Facebook Wall, Meetup Group or Eventbrite event page.

 

How We Share Your Information

We may share your information, including personal information, as follows:

  • TapFuse Users. Your name and any information that you post to the App, including, without limitation, your profile and photo will be available to, and searchable by, users of the App. When you select an event that you are attending, other attendees will have access to your photo and profile to decide whether or not they are interested in connecting with you.
  • Conference and Event Organizers. We may disclose information with conference and event organizers about how their event attendees utilized the App. For example, we may share aggregate information about how many discussions were had, how many meetings were made, or the total number of messages sent during their event. Or, if an even organizer has shared event registration information with us, we may share with the organizer information such as the names, profiles, and connections of our users who attended the event and information about how those users used the App.
  • Conference and Event Corporate Sponsors and Select Third Parties. From time to time, we obtain leads of potential conference attendees from corporate sponsors and other select third parties. We may inform such corporate sponsor or other third party which conferences persons on their particular lead lists attended. We may also share information such as the profiles and connections of our users who attended the event and information about how those users used the App.
  • Service Providers. We may disclose the information we collect from you to third party vendors, service providers, contractors or agents who perform functions on our behalf.

We also may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Business Transfers. If we are acquired by or merged with another company, if substantially all of our assets are transferred to another company, or as part of a bankruptcy proceeding, we may transfer the information we have collected from you to the other company. We also may disclose the information we collect from you in order to comply with the law, a judicial proceeding, court order, or other legal process, such as in response to a court order or a subpoena.
  • To Protect Us and Others. We also may disclose the information we collect from you where we believe it is necessary to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the safety of any person, violations of our Terms of Use or this Policy, or as evidence in litigation in which TapFuse is involved.
  • Aggregate and De-Identified Information. We may share aggregate or de-identified information about users with third parties for marketing, advertising, research or similar purposes.

Security of My Personal Information

We have implemented commercially reasonable precautions to protect the information we collect from loss, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction. Please be aware that despite our best efforts, no data security measures can guarantee 100% security.

You should take steps to protect against unauthorized access to your app account, device, and computer by, among other things, signing off after using a shared computer, choosing an intricate password that nobody else knows or can easily guess, and keeping your log-in and password private to your devices.
